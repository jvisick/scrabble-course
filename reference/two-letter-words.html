The 107 Two-Letter Words

The complete list, grouped by first letter. Your daily 5-minute drill sheet.

Every one of these is legal in North American tournament play (NWL2023). There are exactly 107. Learning all of them is the single highest-return hour of study in Scrabble — they let you play into tight spaces, hook onto other words, and stack parallel plays.

The 8 that win games Master these first — they turn your heaviest tiles into points in cramped spaces: JO QI ZA XI XU AX EX OX
QI and ZA alone are two of the most valuable words in the game — they place a 10- or 5-point tile onto a bonus square for a big score.
