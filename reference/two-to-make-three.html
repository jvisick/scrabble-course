2s to Make 3s — The Hook Chart

Every letter that grows each two-letter word into a three-letter word. Read a row as: front hooks → WORD → back hooks.

A hook is a single letter added to the front or back of a word on the board to make a new word. This chart shows every legal hook of all 107 two-letter words — 850 of the 1,085 three-letter words in NWL2023 are one hook away from a 2. Example row: bcefgjlmoptvwy AR bcefkmost means B​AR, C​AR … are words, and AR​B, AR​C … are words.

The S-question — the 32 that refuse an S

Before you pluralize a 2 in a real game, check it against this list. These do not take an S:

AE AM AN AW AX DE EH ET EW EX HM IS JO LO MM MY NA NE OF OI OK OS OW OX OY SH TO UH US WE XU YO

Famous traps: JO pluralizes as JOES, AX and OX as AXES and OXEN/OXES (4+ letters), and XU is already its own plural — the only 2 with no hooks at all.
